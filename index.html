Matt Sooknah

I am a software engineer with interests in computational biology, machine learning, and the development of new technologies for understanding human disease.

I currently work on the Data Platform team at Calico Labs. Previously, I've worked in the Computational Biology group at 10X Genomics, and in the Data Science & Data Engineering group at the Broad Institute.

I grew up in Rhode Island and studied at MIT, double majoring in physics and ducks. In my spare time, I enjoy running, hiking, reading, and gaming.
